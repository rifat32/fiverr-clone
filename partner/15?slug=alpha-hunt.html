Who Are We?

Alpha Hunt is dedicated to educating our Investor Community before investing with us in hand-selected early-stage projects. Our founding team has invested in blockchain projects since 2017 and knows the importance of sound research before making investment decisions.

We’re always on the hunt for new and exciting projects in the blockchain space to form mutually beneficial working relationships with our growing community.

Investment Philosophy

Our investment philosophy is the same as all key relationships in life, it is people first. When broken down to first principles, it’s the teams vision, discipline, values and capabilities which will determine the success or failure of a project.

Once we’ve assessed the quality of the team, we look for projects which solve a current need in the market in an improved and more efficient manner.

That said, we are always on the look out to support the visionaries in the blockchain space that aim to build the framework of tomorrow in this rapidly evolving ecosystem.

Any investment you make in cryptocurrencies is classified as a high risk investment. When investing in young, start-up projects there is an increased risked so always do your own research before investing. There are many factors at play and many are outside our control.

When an interesting project comes to our attention, we try and get an allocation. If we’re successful, members of Alpha Hunt Club can participate in the offering. To give everyone a fair chance, we always have a minimum and maximum allowed contribution.

𝐖𝐡𝐚𝐭’𝐬 𝐭𝐡𝐞 𝐝𝐢𝐟𝐟𝐞𝐫𝐞𝐧𝐜𝐞 𝐛𝐞𝐭𝐰𝐞𝐞𝐧 𝐇𝐮𝐧𝐭𝐞𝐫𝐬 𝐚𝐧𝐝 𝐈𝐧𝐯𝐞𝐬𝐭𝐨𝐫𝐬?

Hunters is the top tier membership in the Alpha Hunt Club. All allocations are given to this group first and what’s left goes to the investors group. The investors group is free to join and there is a nominal fee to join the Hunters group. When we receive a small allocation for a top project, it will often go exclusively to members of the Hunters.

The fee depends on several factors but we strive to offer some of, if not the lowest fees in the industry. Our fees are 7-10% on average.

𝐖𝐡𝐚𝐭’𝐬 𝐭𝐡𝐞 𝐩𝐫𝐨𝐜𝐞𝐬𝐬 𝐟𝐫𝐨𝐦 𝐩𝐚𝐲𝐦𝐞𝐧𝐭 𝐭𝐨 𝐝𝐢𝐬𝐭𝐫𝐢𝐛𝐮𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧?

We will post new opportunities in a Google form with all the information we’ve gathered about the project. In that form, there is a link to gatherdao which is an industry leading platform to handle payment and fulfilment. You’ll connect your Metamask and send funds to the projects you choose. In the dashboard, you’ll see each project you’ve invested in as well as the status. Once the tokens are distributed, you can claim them directly on the website. Should a project not meet its allocation or things change, you’ll receive a refund which you can also claim there.
