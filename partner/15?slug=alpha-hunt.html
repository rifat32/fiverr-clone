Who Are We?

Alpha Hunt is dedicated to educating our Investor Community before investing with us in hand-selected early-stage projects. Our founding team has invested in blockchain projects since 2017 and knows the importance of sound research before making investment decisions.

Were always on the hunt for new and exciting projects in the blockchain space to form mutually beneficial working relationships with our growing community.

Investment Philosophy

Our investment philosophy is the same as all key relationships in life, it is people first. When broken down to first principles, its the teams vision, discipline, values and capabilities which will determine the success or failure of a project.

Once weve assessed the quality of the team, we look for projects which solve a current need in the market in an improved and more efficient manner.

That said, we are always on the look out to support the visionaries in the blockchain space that aim to build the framework of tomorrow in this rapidly evolving ecosystem.
